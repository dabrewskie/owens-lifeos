## Complete Benefits Catalog (100% P&T)

### 1. Disability Compensation
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Value:** $4,354/mo ($52,248/yr)
- **Notes:** Tax-free. COLA adjusted annually. With 3 dependents and spouse.
- **Action:** Verify dependent additions are current (all 3 kids + Lindsey)

### 2. Chapter 35 — Dependents' Educational Assistance (DEA)
- **Eligibility:** Children of 100% P&T veterans
- **Benefit:** Up to 36 months of education benefits per child
- **Monthly stipend:** ~$1,400/mo (2026 rate, full-time)
- **Age window:** Generally 18-26 years old
- **Applicable to:** Rylan (eligible at 18 = 2030), Emory (2037), Harlan (2041)
- **Total value:** $50,000-100,000+ per child
- **Action:** File VA Form 22-5490 for each child when they turn 18
- **Alternative:** Compare with Post-9/11 GI Bill transfer if applicable
- **CRITICAL:** This is one of the most valuable benefits — worth $150-300K total for 3 kids

### 3. VA Healthcare
- **Eligibility:** Priority Group 1 (100% P&T)
- **Coverage:** Comprehensive healthcare, no copays for most services
- **Value:** $5,000-15,000/yr in avoided healthcare costs
- **Dental:** Not included unless service-connected dental condition
- **Mental Health:** Full PTSD treatment, therapy, medication
- **Action:** Ensure enrolled at local VA medical center. Use for: prescriptions, mental health, annual physicals at minimum

### 4. CHAMPVA (Civilian Health and Medical Program of VA)
- **Eligibility:** Spouse and children of 100% P&T veteran
- **Coverage:** Healthcare coverage for dependents not eligible for TRICARE
- **Cost:** No premiums, minimal copays
- **Coordination:** Secondary to any other health insurance (Lilly/TriMedX plans)
- **Value:** Covers what employer insurance doesn't — can save $2,000-5,000/yr
- **Action:** Apply if not already enrolled. Use as secondary insurance.

### 5. VA Home Loan
- **Eligibility:** Multiple uses, no limit on number of loans
- **Benefits:** No down payment, no PMI, competitive interest rates
- **Funding fee:** WAIVED for 100% P&T (saves 1.25-3.3% of loan amount)
- **Value:** On a $300K home — saves $3,750-9,900 in funding fee alone
- **Action:** Use for next home purchase or refinance. Compare rates regularly.

### 6. Property Tax Deductions (Indiana-Specific)
- **Deduction 1 (IC 6-1.1-12-14):** $24,960 deduction for wartime veteran with 10%+ VA disability
- **Deduction 2 (IC 6-1.1-12-14.5):** $14,000 deduction for totally disabled veteran (assessed value < $240K)
- **Combined:** Up to $38,960 deduction from assessed value (~$779/yr savings at 2% rate)
- **HB 1210 (2026 session):** Increases totally disabled veteran deduction to 100% of assessed value (effective taxes payable 2027). Estimated savings: $3,000-6,000/yr.
- **Application:** File with county auditor (not assessor). Requires VA disability letter + DD-214.
- **Action:** Verify BOTH deductions are filed. Monitor HB 1210 for governor signature.
- **Full analysis:** `~/Library/Mobile Documents/com~apple~CloudDocs/indiana-veteran-tax-benefits.md`

### 7. Vehicle Excise Tax Credit (Indiana)
- **Eligibility:** Veterans whose property tax deduction exceeds assessed value
- **Benefit:** $2 credit per $100 of remaining deduction toward excise tax on up to 2 vehicles (max $70/vehicle)
- **Value:** $70-140/yr
- **CORRECTION:** Indiana does NOT offer free vehicle registration for disabled veterans. Previous entry was inaccurate.
- **Action:** Should be automatically applied if property tax deduction is filed

### 8. State Income Tax Benefits (Indiana)
- **VA disability:** Excluded from Indiana income tax (automatic)
- **Military retirement pay:** 100% exempt from Indiana income tax (since TY2022) — saves ~$3,864/yr at RPED ($63,864/yr x ~4.55% combined state+county rate)
- **Active duty/Guard pay:** Exempt from Indiana income tax (since TY2024) — verify on state return
- **$5,000 military income deduction:** Available for active/reserve component members on IT-40 Schedule 2 (~$228/yr savings)
- **Action:** Verify Guard pay exclusion AND $5,000 deduction are claimed on Indiana return
- **Full analysis:** `~/Library/Mobile Documents/com~apple~CloudDocs/indiana-veteran-tax-benefits.md`

### 9. Commissary and Exchange Privileges
- **Eligibility:** 100% P&T veterans
- **Access:** Military commissaries and exchanges
- **Savings:** 20-30% on groceries vs civilian stores
- **Nearest locations:** [check — likely Camp Atterbury or Grissom ARB area]
- **Action:** Get access card if not already obtained

### 10. Space-Available Travel
- **Eligibility:** 100% P&T veterans
- **Benefit:** Free flights on military aircraft (space permitting)
- **Value:** Varies — can save thousands on family travel
- **Limitations:** Unpredictable schedules, not suitable for time-sensitive travel

### 11. National Parks Lifetime Access Pass
- **Cost:** Free for 100% P&T veterans
- **Value:** $80 (pass cost) + unlimited park entry for lifetime
- **Covers:** All national parks, monuments, recreation areas
- **Action:** Obtain at any national park entrance or online

### 12. Vocational Rehabilitation (Chapter 31)
- **Eligibility:** Service-connected disability with employment barrier
- **Benefit:** Education, training, job placement assistance
- **Note:** May overlap with other education benefits — evaluate
- **Action:** Assess if applicable given current employment status

### 13. Adaptive Housing Grants
- **SAH Grant:** Up to ~$109,000 for home modifications
- **SHA Grant:** Up to ~$21,000 for temporary housing adaptations
- **Eligibility:** Depends on specific disabilities
- **Action:** Evaluate if any home modifications would improve quality of life

### 14. Dependency and Indemnity Compensation (DIC) — For Survivors
- **Not a benefit Tory uses** — this is for Lindsey if Tory dies
- **Value:** ~$1,612/mo to surviving spouse
- **Cross-reference:** estate-guard skill tracks this

### 15. Life Insurance
- **VGLI:** Veterans Group Life Insurance — convert SGLI after separation
- **S-DVI:** Service-Disabled Veterans Insurance
- **Action:** Review current life insurance coverage status
