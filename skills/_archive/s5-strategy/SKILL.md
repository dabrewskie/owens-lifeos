# S5 Plans & Strategy — Long-Range Planning

**Mission:** Everyone plans for tomorrow. S5 plans for 2031 and 2040. The decisions Tory makes this month compound over decades. S5 ensures today's actions serve tomorrow's outcomes.

---

## Why This Exists

Tory's COP has a 90-day horizon. That's tactically excellent and strategically blind. Consider:

- **RPED is September 7, 2040** — 14.5 years away. Every month of savings matters. Every month of NOT saving costs exponentially more later.
- **Director track at Lilly** — 3-5 year timeline. The actions that lead to Director happen 18-24 months before the promotion.
- **Kids' developmental windows** — Rylan is 14. In 4 years she's an adult. The family presence investment has a deadline.
- **Lilly pension** — vests over time. Every year at Lilly adds ~$2,775/mo to retirement income. Leaving early has compounding cost.
- **Health trajectory** — habits at 44 determine health at 64. The body comp work isn't cosmetic — it's longevity infrastructure.

Without S5, these strategic realities get crowded out by today's inbox.

## Strategic Pillars (The 5 Campaign Lines of Effort)

### LOE 1: Financial Independence (RPED 2040)
- **Endstate:** Tory retires Sep 7, 2040 with sufficient passive income
- **Key milestones:** CC payoff (Mar 2026), emergency fund ($47K), max 401k, RPED savings rate
- **Critical path:** $7k+/mo FCF must be deployed systematically, not spent
- **Risk:** Lifestyle inflation absorbs freed cash flow before it's invested

### LOE 2: Career Advancement (Director Track)
- **Endstate:** Director at Lilly within 3-5 years, positioning for VP consideration
- **Key milestones:** High-visibility project, executive sponsor, strategic initiative ownership
- **Critical path:** Shift from executor to strategist — the promotion requires demonstrated strategic thinking, not just operational excellence
- **Risk:** ISTJ comfort with execution prevents the stretch into strategy

### LOE 3: Family Legacy
- **Endstate:** Children launched into adulthood with strong foundation; marriage thriving
- **Key milestones:** Rylan's high school years (4 left), Emory's formative years (now), Harlan's early development
- **Critical path:** Presence is non-negotiable. Estate planning protects everything.
- **Risk:** Career and financial optimization crowd out the actual point of it all

### LOE 4: Health & Longevity
- **Endstate:** Physical platform that supports performance through retirement and beyond
- **Key milestones:** Target body composition, consistent training 4x/week, macro compliance
- **Critical path:** Habits established now determine the trajectory for decades
- **Risk:** PTSD management, stress, and "too busy" narrative erode the foundation

### LOE 5: Personal Development
- **Endstate:** Strategic leader who builds systems, not just executes within them
- **Key milestones:** Personality Hacker completion, Ti Co-Pilot development, executive presence
- **Critical path:** Deliberate growth, not accidental experience
- **Risk:** The ISTJ "I'm fine, I don't need to change" defense
