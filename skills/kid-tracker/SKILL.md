## Screen Time Agreement & Sleep Diary (Rylan)

**Agreement:** `~/Library/Mobile Documents/com~apple~CloudDocs/S1_PERSONNEL_FAMILY/rylan-screen-time-agreement.md`

**Key Commitments (both Rylan and Dad):**
- Screen-free wind-down 1hr before bed
- Phones charge outside the bedroom overnight
- Phones away during family dinners, outings, and 1:1 time
- No hard screen time caps — awareness-based, not restriction-based
- Weekly check-in (Sunday) to adjust what isn't working
- Monthly family review of sleep diary trends

**Sleep Diary Tracking:**
- Rylan logs nightly: bed time, wake time, screens-off time, rested score (1-10), notes
- Format: whatever Rylan prefers (Notes app, paper, shared sheet)
- **Analysis trigger:** When kid-tracker runs, check for sleep diary data and surface patterns:
  - Correlation between screens-off time and rested score
  - Trend in rested score over past 7/30 days
  - Flag if rested score averages below 5 for a week
- **Integration with EOD close:** If Rylan's sleep data is available, include in daily log

**Collaboration Protocol:**
- This is a negotiated agreement, not imposed rules. Rylan has editorial power.
- Dad follows the same commitments. No double standards.
- If any rule makes things worse, it gets changed at the next check-in.
- Quarterly full-reset option — can scrap and rebuild the entire agreement.

**Science Basis (for reference):**
- 78% of ADHD individuals have delayed circadian timing — screens worsen this
- Blue light suppresses melatonin, compounding ADHD sleep dysfunction
- Poor sleep measurably worsens executive function (the core ADHD challenge)
- Hard caps backfire with ADHD teens — autonomy-based approaches show better adherence

## The Standard

These three humans are the reason the system exists. Every financial plan, every career move, every health goal — it all flows back to being a present, capable, engaged father.
